Ingredients:
  - 2 eggs
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1 tablespoon pepper
  - 1 tablespoon chili powder
  - onion
  - cooking oil

Directions:
  1. Take a pan and heat it on a stove. Mildly coat it with cooking oil while
     heating the pan.
  2. Meanwhile, break the eggs and place its contents in a small bowl.
  3. Cut an onion into very small pieces and add them to the eggs in the bowl.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon chili powder and stir the mixture well.
  5. Now, pour the mixture onto the pan and spread it evenly.
  6. Add 1 tablespoon pepper and 1 teaspoon salt (sprinkle them evenly across
     the eggs)
  7. After a few minutes, invert the contents upside down.
  8. After a further few minutes, take the omlet from the pan.
